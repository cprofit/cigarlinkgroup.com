Cigar Link Group

Cigar Link Group is a strategic partnership founded by three independent premium cigar industry consultants. Built on more than 70 years of combined experience across retail, wholesale, manufacturing, sourcing, quality control, brand development and logistics, Cigar Link Group delivers a suite of practical, scalable business solutions for cigar manufacturers, brand owners, retailers and distributors.

The Group’s Mission

At Cigar Link Group, our mission is simple:

To provide practical, transparent and forward‑thinking solutions that help premium cigar businesses operate smarter, scale stronger and grow sustainably.

By combining deep industry knowledge, trusted relationships and a collaborative approach, we eliminate unnecessary complexity and create real‑world opportunities for our factory partners, retail clients and brand owners across the premium cigar marketplace.

Our Services

Cigar Link Group offers a full suite of scalable services tailored for every tier of the premium cigar industry:

  • Business Development & Market Expansion Strategy
  • Retail Display, Merchandising & Sell-Through Optimization
